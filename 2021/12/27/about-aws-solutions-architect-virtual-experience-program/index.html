AWS Solutions Architect Virtual Experience Program

ravi December 27, 2021 2 min read

AWS launch new program !

At AWS, they are eager to give you the chance to start exploring what a career in solutions architecture could look like for you! They are excited to open up the world of solutions architecture as a role to you and teach you the basics so that you can decide if solutions architecture is what you want in your future. 

They are also giving you a sneak peek into the AWS hosting architecture design process whilst also teaching you the fundamentals of our global company. You’ll familiarize yourself with how our solution architects effectively communicate with clients whilst also learning about our billing structures which are key to providing commercial solutions for our clients all over the world. 

What’s in it for you?

  • Ability to take our program in your own time, at your own pace.
  • Gain marketable skills and experience you can build off of.
  • Certificate of completion you can put on your resume or your LinkedIn Profile to elevate your employability.
  • See if a career in solutions architecture is the right fit for you!

Why join this virtual experience program?

At AWS, we’re eager to give you the chance to start exploring what a career in solutions architecture could look like for you! We are excited to open up the world of solutions architecture as a role to you, and teach you the basics so that you can decide if solutions architecture is what you want in your future. 

We are giving you a sneak peak into the AWS hosting architecture design process whilst also teaching you the fundamentals of our global company. You’ll familiarize yourself with how our solution architects effectively communicate with clients whilst also learning about our billing structures which are key to providing commercial solutions for our clients all over the world. 

What’s in it for you: 

•  Ability to take our program in your own time, at your own pace.
•  Gain marketable skills and experience you can build off of.
•  Certificate of completion you can put on your resume or your Linkedin to elevate your employability.

Perks of this Program

  • Do it in your own time
  • Gain real work Experience
  • Make your CV and LinkedIn Profile stand-out
  • Learn Direct from AWS
  • Discover if this career is for you
  • Set yourself for the success

Certificate

Yes, you get a free verified Certificate from Amazon AWS. This program is self-paced. It takes 1-2 hours to complete this virtual experience program.
